Sustainable Money

An exploration of what it takes - and whether it is possible - to balance the growth of a strong economy with environmental sustainability and social responsibility.

Through reading of books, academic journals, the news, and other sources I hope to have a thoughtful conversation about the following topics (and more):

  • What is environmental economics and how can it be used to influence and understand the impacts of human consumption?
  • If everyone were to stop hyper consumerism of single use and non-renewable goods, what would happen to the economy? Globally? Locally?
  • How can the dogmas of minimalism and financial independence be used together to inform a path towards environmental sustainability?
  • What is our social responsibility to consume less or more or in certain ways?

I don't pretend to be an expert on any of these topics, nor do I expect to have an unwavering answer to any of these. The goal of this site will be to have a semi-academic, semi-reflective conversation about how to earn and spend money sustainability both at a micro and macro economic scale.
